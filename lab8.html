Get the vaccine

"Vaccination is one of the most important public health measures to prevent epidemics. Vaccines help the body produce antibodies against disease-causing viruses and bacteria. Widespread vaccination will help achieve community immunity, preventing outbreaks and spread of epidemics. Everyone should be fully vaccinated according to the recommended schedule to protect their own health and the community’s health. Parents need to ensure their children are vaccinated on schedule to prevent illnesses. Get vaccinated to protect yourself and those around you!"

Consulting your health

Health care is extremely important. To maintain good health, you should have regular health checkups, exercise, eat healthy, manage stress, and get enough sleep. If you have any health issues, you should see a doctor or healthcare professional for advice. They will evaluate your symptoms, examine you, and accurately diagnose your health condition. After that, you will receive recommendations on treatments, medications or proper health care. Always follow the doctor's instructions to quickly recover your health.

Certified Nurses

Certified Nurses are nurses who have passed the certification exam administered by the National Nursing Council to evaluate their professional knowledge and clinical experience. Having a certification affirms the nurse's expertise in a specific specialty such as neonatology, critical care, surgery, etc. This serves as evidence that they have attained a high level of professional competence and are committed to delivering quality healthcare services. Certified nurses tend to garner more trust and respect.

Covid-19 Hospitals

During the Covid-19 pandemic, hospitals play a crucial role in treating patients infected with the novel coronavirus. Temporary hospitals dedicated to Covid-19 patients were constructed to prevent the spread of the epidemic. These hospitals are fully equipped with infrastructure and medical staff to treat a large number of patients. The team of doctors, nurses here must strictly comply with personal protective regulations to ensure safety. The effective operation of Covid-19 hospitals helps minimize mortality and control the epidemic.
